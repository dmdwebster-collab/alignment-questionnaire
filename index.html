Alignment Questionnaire

This questionnaire assesses alignment between your understanding and how the practice operates. It is not a test — where gaps exist, that tells management where we need to communicate more clearly.

Important

  • Do not look anything up — no practice software, staff handbook, online research, or prior documents.
  • If unsure, say so — that is a perfectly valid and useful response.
